Iran:

State Sponsor of Terrorism

Iran is the second most populous country in the Middle East.
Iran is made up of Persians and other ethnic groups. Farsi is the most common language.
The Iranian people have been struggling towards democracy for more than a century.
Iran’s Islamic government is the world’s largest state sponsor of terrorism.
The regime regularly violates human rights, discriminating against women and gay people, and persecuting religious and ethnic minorities.
