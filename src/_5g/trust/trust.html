Trust
Who will you trust to control your 5G?
You need to be able to trust that 5G equipment and software companies will not threaten your national security, privacy, intellectual property, or human rights. Trust cannot exist where telecom vendors are subject to an authoritarian government, like the People’s Republic of China that lacks an independent judiciary or rule of law that would prevent this misuse of data. Further, ownership of Huawei is not transparent, and has a history of unethical and illegal behavior, including IP theft.
